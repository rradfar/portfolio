About Me

I am a front-end web developer

I am an Iranian/Canadian developer living in Vancouver, Canada. I have a Bachelor's degree in Computer Science from the University of Victoria. I have about a year and a half of professional web development experience being a part of the Fibre Internet team at Telus. Telus is a large telecommunication corporation of over 60,000 employees headquartered in Vancouver.

On a day-to-day basis, I used Twig (a PHP template language), SCSS, and JavaScript within CodeIgniter (a PHP framework) to convert the designs handed off to me by our UX and UI designers into webpages. I also learned some PHP in the process.

I am currently in the process of learning React.js.
